The gas turbine crunch

The podcast episode discusses the current state and future outlook of the gas turbine market, focusing on lead times, pricing, market dynamics, and industry trends. It highlights the impact of factors like raw material costs, aerospace industry demands, and oil and gas market cycles on gas turbine production. The conversation delves into the evolving landscape of gas turbine usage in electricity generation, including varying scales of projects and market drivers like grid-scale battery storage, renewable energy expansion, data center growth, and natural gas availability. The experts emphasize the efforts to manage lead times, address pricing challenges, and adapt to changing market demands across different segments of the gas turbine industry.

How geothermal gets built

Latitude Media is hosting a transition AI conference on June 12, 2025, focusing on energy infrastructure in the AI-driven load growth era. Speakers include representatives from FERVO Energy, Form Energy, and more. What to make of Trump's deep-sea minerals push

Latitude Media's Transition AI conference on June 12, 2025 in Boston will focus on energy infrastructure in the era of AI-driven load growth. A former race car engineer on battery safety and supply chains [partner content]

In this podcast, Tristan Doherty shares his journey from pursuing computer engineering to becoming a race engineer for an IndyCar team.
