The story of steam

The podcast discusses the importance of steam in industrial processes and the challenges of decarbonizing steam production. It explores options like electrifying boilers with resistive or electrode boilers and using heat pumps, with a focus on standardizing and productizing heat pump solutions. The host and guest delve into the complexities of utilizing waste heat, cautioning that it may not always be as straightforward or cost-effective as it seems. The episode also features sponsors ANZA and EnergyHub, highlighting their energy storage and grid flexibility solutions.

Podcast 2025-06-05

The gas turbine crunch

The podcast discusses the current state of the gas turbine market, focusing on key players like Mitsubishi, Siemens, and GE Vernova. It explores historical boom and bust cycles, emphasizing factors like Enron's artificial demand and deregulation. The conversation delves into market dynamics, supply chain complexities, and the impact of aerospace industry demands on gas turbine production. The discussion also touches on rising costs due to raw material price increases and the efforts of OEMs to manage lead times, which currently range from 36 to 48 months. Market drivers such as grid-scale battery storage, plant retirements, renewable energy expansion, data center growth, and natural gas availability are highlighted as influencing turbine demand across different size categories.

How geothermal gets built

Latitude Media is hosting the Transition AI conference on June 12, 2025, focusing on energy infrastructure in the AI-driven load growth era.
